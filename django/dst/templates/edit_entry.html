  • How do I make bold, italic, lists, etc?
{% include "view_entry.html" with entry=form.instance is_preview=True %}

Put asterisks around a word for italics: *alpha* is rendered as alpha

Put double asterisks around a word for bold: **beta** is rendered as beta

Make bullet lists with asterisks, like this: 

* an item
* another item
* final item

Make numbered lists with numbers, like this (the 1s will be replaced with the correct numbers): 

1. the first
1. the second
1. the third

If you want a nested list, or sub-list, put four spaces in front of the number or asterisk, like this: 

1. the first
    1. sub-item
    2. second sub-item
    3. third sub-item
2. the second

For even more formatting options, read the docs here.
